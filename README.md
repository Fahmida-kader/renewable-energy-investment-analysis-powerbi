# Renewable Energy Investment Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes renewable energy investment opportunities by combining electricity market trends, energy production data, and financial feasibility indicators.

The dashboard helps users explore historical electricity prices, forecasted market demand, investment feasibility, net present value (NPV), and energy mix distribution to support data-driven renewable energy investment decisions.

## Key Features

- Interactive date range filtering
- Electricity price forecasting analysis
- Historical electricity price trend monitoring
- Renewable energy mix distribution visualization
- Investment feasibility assessment
- Net Present Value (NPV) analysis
- Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE) evaluation
- Market demand insights

## KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Historical Electricity Price | Average historical electricity prices |
| Energy Market Demand | Average market demand |
| Net Present Value (NPV) | Financial viability indicator |
| LCOE | Levelized Cost of Energy |
| Investment Feasibility | Investment attractiveness score |
